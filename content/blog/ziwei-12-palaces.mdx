## A Map of Life

<GlossaryTooltip term="Zi Wei Dou Shu">Zi Wei Dou Shu</GlossaryTooltip> (<PinyinBadge pinyin="zǐ wēi dǒu shù" />), or Purple Star Astrology, is one of the most sophisticated systems of Chinese metaphysics. Its core structure is the **12 Palaces** — each governing a distinct domain of life.

## The Twelve Palaces

| # | Palace | Chinese | Governs |
|---|--------|---------|---------|
| 1 | **Life (Self)** | 命宫 | Core personality, life direction, overall fortune |
| 2 | **Parents** | 父母宫 | Relationship with parents, authority figures, mentors |
| 3 | **Blessings** | 福德宫 | Inner happiness, spiritual life, enjoyment, mental health |
| 4 | **Property** | 田宅宫 | Home, real estate, living environment, family roots |
| 5 | **Career** | 官禄宫 | Profession, work, public life, achievements |
| 6 | **Friends** | 交友宫 | Friendships, subordinates, colleagues, supporters |
| 7 | **Travel** | 迁移宫 | External environment, relocation, outward expression |
| 8 | **Health** | 疾厄宫 | Physical health, illness, accidents |
| 9 | **Wealth** | 财帛宫 | Money, income, spending habits, financial strategy |
| 10 | **Children** | 子女宫 | Children, creativity, pleasure, sexual life |
| 11 | **Spouse** | 夫妻宫 | Marriage, romantic partnerships, relationship patterns |
| 12 | **Siblings** | 兄弟宫 | Siblings, close peers, collaborators, competition |

## Reading the Palaces

The 12 palaces do not sit in isolation. Each palace forms a **triangle** with two others, creating a dynamic field of influence. For example, Life-Spouse-Career form one such triad — your personality, your marriage, and your work are deeply intertwined.

Every chart has stars distributed across these palaces. The **Zi Wei (Purple Star)** is the emperor star — its placement and companions in a palace signal special emphasis in that life area.

## Self-Discovery Through Zi Wei

Unlike <GlossaryTooltip term="Ba Zi">Ba Zi</GlossaryTooltip>, which excels at timing and elemental balance, Zi Wei is unmatched for **detailed life-area analysis**. A skilled practitioner can read from your chart:

- Your natural career inclinations
- Relationship patterns and timing
- Financial strengths and risks
- Health vulnerabilities
- The overall arc of your life's fortune
